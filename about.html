About me


Hi, I'm Damien, your dedicated running coach. With a passion for running and a commitment to helping others achieve their goals, I specialize in guiding both beginners and intermediate runners through their journey—from completing their first 5K to conquering the marathon distance.

My coaching philosophy is simple: every runner is unique, and I tailor my approach to fit your individual needs, pace, and lifestyle. Whether you're looking to build endurance, improve your speed, or simply enjoy the process of running, I'm here to provide the support and expertise you need.

With years of experience in the running community, I understand the challenges and triumphs that come with training. My goal is to empower you with the knowledge, motivation, and personalized training plans that will help you reach your personal best.

Let's lace up our shoes and hit the road together—your running journey starts here!
